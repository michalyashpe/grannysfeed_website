Granny's Feed

A free, easy and fun way to make your granny smile

Granny's feed is the way to keep in touch with your granny easily. All the family can share photos in real time so your granny can catch up from her sofa. She doens't need to know ANYTHING about computers.

Step One:

Create granny’s dropbox account

Step Two

create folder and share with whole family

Step Three

install local dropbox folder on granny’s computer

Step Four

Configure screen saver to screen random pictures from dropbox folder

Step Five

Configure computer to never sleep

Step Six

Share your pictures to granny and watch her smile
